04/03/2018

Andy Warhol is one of my favorite artists to ever walk this planet. I was first introduced to him during my first visit to the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA) in New York City. My Graphic Design History professor showed our class this vintage Esquire magazine cover that shows Warhol drowning in a can of Campbell's Soup. I thought the concept was satirical yet genius. Creative Direction by George Lois.
