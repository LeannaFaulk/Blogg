I met with Matthew Knopp, owner of Tattoo Paradise, in Adams Morgan this semester. I was fortuante enough to interview him about being a tattoo artist in the DMV and he came to own three of the most popular tattoo shops in the area. I filmed the interview with a Canon Vixia Camcorder with an attached microphone. The interview was edited via Adobe Premiere.
